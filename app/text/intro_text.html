Description:

An online tool capable of estimating the maximum daily number of COVID-19 cases your healthcare system could sustain based on age-based case distribution and severity and the number of appropriable acute and critical care resources.

How to use this tool:
  1. First, review the default age-based case distribution and severity, which is based on US data from the CDC.
    • Optional: You may enter your own custom information into the table based on local clinical data and experience.
  2. Second, tune the inputs for Expected Length of Stay for COVID-19 Cases and Resource Availability for COVID-19 Cases to your own environment.
  3. Third, inspect the results in the bar graph, which presents the maximum daily number of COVID-19 cases your healthcare system could sustain based on acute care beds, critical care beds, and mechanical ventilators.
  4. Fourth, visualize how the results change when modifying the input parameters.
  5. Fifth, generate a report to keep a copy of the results!
