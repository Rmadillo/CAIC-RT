COVID-19 Hospitalization Tool

Authors

  • Vasily Giannakeas, MPH
  • Nathan M. Stall, MD
  • Deepit Bhatia, MPH
  • Matthew T. Warkentin, MSc

Objective: To develop an online tool capable of estimating maximal allowable daily cases of COVID-19 that health and critical care systems could sustain given age-based case distributions of COVID-19.

How to Use This Tool:

  1. First, select the releavant age distribution from the countries provided
    • Optional: You may enter your own custom table!
  2. Second, tune the Length of Stay and Resource Availability parameters to your own envrioment
  3. Three, visualize how the results will change under different contexts
  4. Fourth, generate a report to keep a copy of the results!
